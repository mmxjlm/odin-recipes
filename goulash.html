Goulash

Descirption

Goulash is a very delicious italian type dinner. It is fairly inexpensive to make and it yields several servings.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. italian style bulk sausage
  • 2 cans italian style diced tomatoes
  • 1 can tomato sauce
  • 1 onion
  • 1 green pepper
  • 1 can mushrooms strained (optional)
  • 1 box elbow macaroni
  1. Dice green peppers and onions into uniform pieces about 1/8 inch wide.
  2. Add diced vegatables and mushrooms to pan and brown the italian sausage until fully cooked.
  3. Boil macaroni noodles according to package.
  4. Strain noodles and add all ingredients back into the pot that the noodles were cooked in.
  5. Stir and let sit for 5 minutes and serve.
