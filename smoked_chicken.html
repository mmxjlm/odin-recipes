Smoked Chicken

Description

This is my all time favorite food to cook. It takes some time but the pay off is worth it. If you have never smoked anything before this is a very forgiving meat to make. The recipe is listed below:

  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 1 Tablespoon salt
  • 1 Tablespoon pepper
  • 2 Tablespoons Garlic Powder
  • 1 Tablespoon Paprika
  1. First thing you are going to want to do is bring your chicken to room temperature by letting it sit on the counter for about 45 minutes.
  2. While the chicken is being temped you can combine all the ingredients into a bowl and mix together with a fork or whisk.
  3. After the chicken is temped rub the ingredients over and under the skin. Don't be afraid to use all of it, the flavor will not be overbearing in the end.
  4. When you are done coating the chicken heat your smoker to 225 degrees Farenheit.
  5. Place chicken on the rack and smoke it until the internal temp at the thickest part of the breast is 165 degrees Farenheit.
  6. Pull the chicken out and let it sit for about 15 to 20 minutes to let the juices settle back into the meat.
  7. After it has rested carve the chicken and enjoy!
